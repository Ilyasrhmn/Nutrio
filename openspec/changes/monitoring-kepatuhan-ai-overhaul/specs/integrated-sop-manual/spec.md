## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Full SOP Knowledge Base
The system SHALL provide a comprehensive digital manual covering end-to-end operational procedures within the portal.

#### Scenario: Navigating Manual Tabs
- **WHEN** user clicks on the "Pusat Panduan & Kepatuhan (SOP)" menu
- **THEN** the system SHALL display tabs for "Kamus Pinalti", "Panduan Foto AI", and "Alur Checkpoint"

### Requirement: Contextual Rule Explanation
The system SHALL provide on-screen help icons next to score and penalty elements.

#### Scenario: Rule Discovery
- **WHEN** user hovers over a penalty item in the daily history
- **THEN** a tooltip SHALL appear with the exact SOP reference and logic for the deduction
